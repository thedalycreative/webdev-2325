Email clients/colleagues with a summary of the QA findings.
  • Include screenshots or screen recordings of any issues found
  • Call out any critical issues that need immediate attention
  • Offer to schedule a follow-up meeting to discuss fixes and next steps
Best Practices:
  1. Identify The Issue(s)
  2. Reproduce The Issue(s)
  3. Screenshot/Record The Issue(s)
  4. Offer A Solution
  5. Communicate Clearly and Professionally
    • Use clear, non-technical language when explaining issues to clients/colleagues
    • Be concise and to the point, avoiding unnecessary jargon
  6. Follow Up - After sending the initial communication, follow up to ensure the client/colleague received the information
