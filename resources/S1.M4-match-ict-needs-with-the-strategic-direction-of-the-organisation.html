Case Study: ABC Solutions

Background:
ABC Solutions is an ICT company known for its software development and IT services. Their current website sees many visitors each day however was built 10 years ago and is built from simple HTML and CSS.

Strategic Plan:
ABC Solutions' strategic plan is centered around becoming a leader in the IT industry, driving innovation, and delivering transformative solutions while upholding core values of integrity, collaboration, and customer focus. With a vision to pioneer in the field, ABC Solutions aims to provide cutting-edge IT solutions that empower businesses and individuals alike.

Strategic/Organisational Objectives:

  • Showcase expertise and innovation
  • Attract more clients and increase revenue
  • Stand out as an innovative leader
  • Ensure client data security and trust
  • Have an innovative and up-to-date website

Industry Environment:
ABC Solutions operates in the dynamic and rapidly evolving Information and Communication Technology (ICT) industry. Key factors include technological advancements, highly competitive landscape, and regulatory compliance requirements for data privacy and cybersecurity.

Current Systems:

  • Website Software: HTML 4 and CSS 2 (approximately 20 years old)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Hosting: Dedicated server

Part 1: Evaluate Current Strategic Plan

Question 1: Summarise the organisation's current strategic plan (list the main points)
  • Become a market leader, pioneer innovation, and drive digital transformation.
  • Uphold integrity, collaboration, and customer focus.
  • Foster talent development and stay at the forefront of technology.
  • Ensure customer satisfaction.
Question 2: How does the strategic plan align with the current industry environment and organisational objectives?

The plan's goal of innovation aligns with industry demands and organisational objectives, but the current 10-year-old ICT systems directly contradicts the vision of being an innovative leader.

Question 3: What software systems are used?

HTML 4 and CSS 2.

Question 4: What data management systems are used?

MySQL

Question 5: What website hosting platform is used?

Dedicated Server.

Question 6: List any gaps or improvement opportunities between the strategic plan and current ICT systems

The critical gap is that the antique website technology fails to showcase expertise, hinders client attraction, and poses a major security risk, contradicting every strategic objective.

Question 7: Write out a proposal for changes to the organisation's ICT systems
  1. Full Website Redesign/Redevelopment: Migrate to a modern CMS (e.g., WordPress/Headless) using current responsive frameworks.
  2. Infrastructure & Hosting Upgrade: Migrate to Managed Cloud Hosting for better security, scalability, and performance.
  3. Security Overhaul: Implement HTTPS/SSL and modern security protocols.

Part 2: Evaluate Effects of Changes

Question 8: Do the proposed ICT changes support what the organisation wants to achieve in the long run?

Yes. The changes provide the necessary digital foundation (security, speed, innovation) to achieve all long-term strategic goals (market leadership, innovation, client trust).

Question 9: What will the potential impacts be of implementing the proposed changes?

Positive: Increased revenue, brand credibility, more traffic to website.

Negative: More upfront cost, downtime during migration, staff training.

Question 10: How will the proposed ICT changes contribute to helping the organisation reach its goals?

More traffic: will function as a 24/7 innovative sales tool, tangibly demonstrating expertise and improving client security and conversion rates.

Question 11: Evaluate the difficulty of implementing the proposed changes. What difficulties could you encounter? (List 2)
  • Data & Content Migration
  • Staff Resistance/Training
  • Budget/Scope

Question 15: Are there any industry best practices or regulatory requirements that need to be adhered to?

Regulatory: GDPR / Privacy Act compliance for data security and handling.

Best Practice: WCAG Accessibility standards (Level AA) and use of modern semantic web standards (HTML5).

Question 16: What are the key performance indicators (KPIs) or metrics aimed to be achieved?
  • Conversion Rate: Increase by 20% (more clients/revenue)
  • Page Speed Score: Achieve Mobile Performance Score of 80+ (showcase expertise)
  • Security Vulnerabilities: Zero critical/high vulnerabilities post-launch (client trust)
Question 17: How will the schedule be monitored throughout the implementation process?
  • Use a Project Management Tool (Trello/Jira/Slack/Monday) to track tasks
  • Weekly Milestone Reviews to check progress against the schedule
  • Maintain a formal Risk Register & Status Reports from Developers
